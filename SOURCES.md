# Sources & References

## Foundational Books

### Academic & Scientific
- Moody, R. (1975). *Life After Life*. Mockingbird Books. ISBN: 978-0062517395
  - **Seminal work that coined the term "NDE" and systematized study**
- van Lommel, P. (2010). *Consciousness Beyond Life: The Science of the Near-Death Experience*. HarperOne. ISBN: 978-0061777264
- Greyson, B. (2021). *After: A Doctor Explores What Near-Death Experiences Reveal about Life and Beyond*. St. Martin's Essentials. ISBN: 978-1250263193
- Ring, K. (1980). *Life at Death: A Scientific Investigation of the Near-Death Experience*. Coward, McCann & Geoghegan.
- Sabom, M. (1982). *Recollections of Death: A Medical Investigation*. Harper & Row.
- Parnia, S. (2013). *Erasing Death: The Science That Is Rewriting the Boundaries Between Life and Death*. HarperOne. ISBN: 978-0062080608

### Cross-Cultural Studies
- Kellehear, A. (1996). *Experiences Near Death: Beyond Medicine and Religion*. Oxford University Press.
- Becker, C.B. (1993). *Paranormal Experience and Survival of Death*. State University of New York Press.

## Peer-Reviewed Scientific Papers

### Major Studies

#### The Lancet Study
- van Lommel, P., van Wees, R., Meyers, V., & Elfferich, I. (2001). "Near-death experience in survivors of cardiac arrest: a prospective study in the Netherlands." *The Lancet*, 358(9298), 2039-2045.
  - **Prospective study of 344 cardiac arrest patients; 18% reported NDEs**

#### AWARE Study
- Parnia, S., et al. (2014). "AWARE—AWAreness during REsuscitation—A prospective study." *Resuscitation*, 85(12), 1799-1805.

### Other Scientific Papers
- Greyson, B. (1983). "The Near-Death Experience Scale: Construction, reliability, and validity." *Journal of Nervous and Mental Disease*, 171, 369-375.
- Greyson, B. (2010). "Implications of Near-Death Experiences for a Postmaterialist Psychology." *Psychology of Religion and Spirituality*, 2(1), 37-45.
- Agrillo, C. (2011). "Near-death experience: Out-of-body and out-of-brain?" *Review of General Psychology*, 15(1), 1-10.
- Holden, J.M., Greyson, B., & James, D. (Eds.) (2009). *The Handbook of Near-Death Experiences: Thirty Years of Investigation*. Praeger.

## Neurological & Skeptical Perspectives

### Neurological Explanations
- Strassman, R. (2001). *DMT: The Spirit Molecule*. Park Street Press. ISBN: 978-0892819270
  - Explores DMT and its similarity to NDE experiences
- Blackmore, S. (1993). *Dying to Live: Near-Death Experiences*. Prometheus Books.
  - Skeptical neurological perspective
- Nelson, K.R., et al. (2006). "Does the arousal system contribute to near death experience?" *Neurology*, 66(7), 1003-1009.
  - REM intrusion hypothesis

### Critical Analysis

- French, C.C. (2005). "Near-death experiences in cardiac arrest survivors." *Progress in Brain Research*, 150, 351-367.
